The Only Active MegaGame Group in California is back with our newest MegaGame: Cruise Control!
You and your group of supernatural companions have all decided to take some time off from the main plot-lines of your various stories for a bit of much-deserved down-time. A "beach episode." if you will. Finally some R&R from all that crime fighting/evil-doing/world domination plots/Mystery Solving/Ghost-busting hectic life of the day-to-day, relaxing away from the civilian populace. Time to kick back and reeeeeee-LAX. Luckily, you and your friends scored a great deal on a cruise ship that both knows your secret life, and caters to your special needs.

But what are the odds that your sworn enemies would book the exact same cruise as you at the exact same time, while also on their vacation?

Twelve teams find themselves all stuck on the same cruise ship, each torn between hunting their nemesis and defeating them ONCE AND FOR ALL or actually having a relaxing vacation. Can your team leave work behind you? Or will you spend your whole vacation conjuring Cthulu's Nephew to drown those meddlesome CIA agents?

Featuring:

  • 3 hours of trading, negotiation, and chaos
  • Vampires bickering about work/life balance
  • 60 players, 12 teams, each with individual objectives
  • Unfolding Narrative Centered around Nautical Nonsense

Admission also includes access to Game Diego's BoardGame Collection from 10PM to closing.

This is a 3 hour light roleplaying MegaGame that is designed for brand new and experienced players.

Factions in play:
  • A League of Snobbish Vampires
  • The Cult of Impending Doom/Dark God Fan Club
  • Mysterious Yet Menacing Government Agents
  • Monster Hunters Inc.
  • The Smalltown Sheriff's Department
  • Spunky Reporters from The Daily Gazette
  • A Conspiracy Theorist Forum Moderator team
  • The Elder Council of Elderly Eldrich Gods
  • The Gang of Meddling Kids
  • Leadership of the local Faceless Horrors & Aberrations Union
  • The Completely Normal Roswell Family who is NOT FROM SPACE.
  • A Confused family Who Thought this was a Disney Cruise

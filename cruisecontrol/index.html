The Only Active MegaGame Group in California is back with our newest MegaGame:Cruise Control!
You and your group of supernatural companions have all decided to take a relaxing break on a cruise line that caters to your...proclivities. Finally some R&R from all that crime fighting/evildoing, relaxing away from the civilian populace. But what are the odds that your sworn enemies would book the exact same cruise as you at the exact same time?

Twelve teams, each having a deep and personal hatred for each other all find themselves torn from wanting to finally defeat their nemesis once and for all, or JUST RELAX BUFFY. YOU ARE ON VACATION. KILL THE VAMPIRE LATER. DON'T BLOW OUR VAY-CAY. WE'RE NOT GETTING THE DEPOSIT BACK.

Featuring:

  • 3 hours of trading, negotiation, and chaos
  • Two floors of exploration of a unique event space
  • 60 players, 12 teams, each with individual objectives
  • Unfolding Narrative Centered around Nautical Nonsense

Admission also includes access to Game Diego's BoardGame Collection from 10PM to closing.

This is a 3 hour light roleplaying MegaGame that is designed for brand new and experienced players.

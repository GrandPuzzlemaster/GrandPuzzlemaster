Game Diego 4

Boardgame & MegaGame Convention

October 5th, 2024
10AM to 11PM

Handerly Hotel
950 Hotel Circle North, San Diego, CA 92108

Our biggest event yet! Game Diego is a one-day board game convention in Downtown San Diego. For the first time, we are going to be at the Handerly Hotel for a day of board games, card games, roleplaying games, and more!

First 40 tickets get a special hand-made Commemorative Badge/Coaster. Hurry: Window closes Friday, September 20th.

Featuring:

  • The debut run of Cruise Control, our brand-new 60-player MegaGame
  • Game Library of hundreds of hand-selected high-quality games for all skill levels, with friendly people to teach them!
  • Lots of other people who will want to play board/card/Roleplaying games with you.
  • Cash Bar and Snacks (Healthy and Otherwise) will be available!

Useful Details:

You are encouraged to bring your own games! But please attend to your possessions. We are not responsible for lost or stolen items. Plenty of on-site parking! The Handerly Hotel will have discounted parking available at $2/hour or $10 for the day. Hotel policy disallows outside food or drink. Both will be available for purchase at the convention! Tickets will cost $30 at the door. Admission to Cruise Control day-of is not impossible, but is unlikely.

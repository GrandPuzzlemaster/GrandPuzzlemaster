Game Diego Summer

MegaGame & boardgame Convention

June 10th, 2023

Tenth Avenue Arts Center, Downtown San Diego

Our biggest event ever! Game Diego is a one-day board game convention in Downtown San Diego. We're taking over the entire Tenth Avenue Arts Center for a day of board games, card games, roleplaying games, and more!

Featuring:

  • Hear/Say: The Final Curtain. Murder Mystery MegaGame!
  • Drinks and Hot Food!
  • Hundreds of the latest BoardGames available for free rental!
  • ElectraCity V0.2: Puzzle Hunt
  • Micro-golf course
  • Hosted Events

We are as welcoming as possible to new gamers! Volunteer teachers will be around to explain games and organize things.
